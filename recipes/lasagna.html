Lasagna

Description

This gooey lasagna will transport you to the hills of Tuscany. Pair it with your favorite Chianti and you'll be dreaming of Giuseppes tonight.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Carrots
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Set oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Remove your oven rack and dump all ingredients onto the bottom of the oven
  3. Pray to the lasagna gods
  4. Enjoy!
